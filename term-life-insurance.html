Answers

Term Life Insurance questions.

What happens when my term ends?
Coverage stops, or continues at a higher annual rate. Many policies can convert to permanent before a deadline.
Do I need a medical exam?
Often no — many carriers offer accelerated, no-exam underwriting for qualified applicants.
Is the payout taxed?
Death benefits are generally income-tax-free to your beneficiaries.
Can I have more than one policy?
Yes — laddering different amounts and terms is common and smart.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Term has no surrender penalty.
