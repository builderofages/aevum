# Aevum Brand System

**Name:** Aevum — Latin for "a lifetime; eternity." The philosophical term for the mode of existence between time and eternity. Pronounced **EE-vum** (AY-vum acceptable).

**One-liner:** Life insurance with a real advisor. Decided in minutes, not months.

**Position:** The anti-funnel. Digital speed + a named human advisor. Never scammy, never a call center, never a bait quote.

## Voice

- Plain, confident, warm. Short sentences. No insurance jargon without translation.
- Promises are about *our behavior*, never policy outcomes (compliance + honesty).
- Say the quiet part: how we're paid, why rates change, when work coverage IS enough.
- Founder is a working business owner (roofing company) — owner-to-owner credibility is a core asset. Use it.

## Color

| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Ink | #12182B | primary dark (near-black navy), hero, footer bands |
| Ink-2 | #1C2440 | elevated dark surfaces |
| Paper | #FAF8F4 | warm off-white background (never pure white) |
| Oat | #F1EDE4 | alternate section background |
| Brass | #B08D46 | THE accent — rules, numerals, primary CTA. Use sparingly |
| Brass-deep | #8F7134 | brass on light backgrounds (contrast-safe) |
| Text | #2A2E3D | body text |

Forbidden: teal, blue-purple gradients, pure #000/#FFF, more than one accent.

## Type

- **Headlines:** Fraunces (Google Fonts, variable) — 400-600, letter-spacing -0.02em, text-wrap: balance
- **Body/UI:** Instrument Sans — 400/500/600, 16-17px body, 1.5-1.6 line-height
- Never: Inter-default look, all-bold walls, more than 2 families

## Imagery

Documentary warmth: real families and real work in natural light, 35mm film grade, warm neutrals with ink-navy shadows. Never: stock posing, 3D renders, AI-face gloss, impossibly lit offices. Current assets are Higgsfield-generated placeholders — replace progressively with real photography of the actual advisor, office, and (with releases) clients.

## Motion

200-400ms, ease-out, one signature moment max per page, prefers-reduced-motion always respected. Motion communicates state; it never decorates.

## Trust furniture (place at decision points, with context)

- "We quote only carriers rated A- or better by AM Best"
- License lines + NPN in footer
- Real reviews with AggregateRating schema (once they exist — never fabricate)
- Named advisor with real photo
