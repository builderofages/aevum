Answers

Every hesitation, answered straight.

Is that instant estimate real?
Yes — a genuine first-pass from your answers and current carrier rate logic, not a teaser that triples later. It's not a final offer (only the carrier sets your exact rate through underwriting), but most healthy applicants land close.
Do I need a medical exam?
Often no. Many carriers approve healthy applicants through accelerated underwriting using data checks instead of bloodwork — frequently same-day. With health conditions, we route you to the product most likely to approve you.
What if I've been turned down before?
You still have options. Simplified-issue and guaranteed-acceptance plans exist for harder-to-insure families — within the eligible age band, guaranteed issue cannot decline you.
How do you get paid?
The carrier pays us a commission — you pay the same premium either way. We're independent, so we shop 40+ carriers for your best fit instead of pushing one product.
Which states do you serve?
Florida, Maryland, Virginia and Pennsylvania. Products and rates vary by state and are confirmed by the issuing carrier.
