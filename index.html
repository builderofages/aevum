Straight answers

You've got questions.
Here's the truth.

Is that instant price real?

It's a genuine estimate built from the answers you gave and current carrier rate tables — not a made-up teaser. It's not a final offer, because only the insurance carrier can set your exact rate through underwriting. But it's an honest ballpark, and most healthy applicants land close to it.

Do I need a medical exam?

Most healthy applicants qualify for no-exam (accelerated) underwriting and get a decision fast — sometimes the same day. When a case needs full underwriting, it usually means we're chasing you a better rate class, and we'll tell you exactly why it's worth it.

I was declined online before. Am I done?

No — this is honestly our favorite kind of case. Instant-decision algorithms reject anything they can't neatly score. A human advisor with 40+ carriers, including ones that specialize in specific health histories, very often finds coverage the algorithm couldn't.

How does Aevum get paid?

Like every agency, the carrier pays us a commission when a policy is placed — it's already baked into the premium and doesn't change your price versus going direct. The difference: we're independent, so our only incentive is putting you with the right carrier, not the only one we represent.

Is Aevum an insurance company?

No — we're an independent, licensed insurance agency. Your policy is issued by a third-party carrier and all guarantees are backed solely by that carrier. Our job is to shop the market for you, fight through underwriting, and stay your advisor long after the sale.
