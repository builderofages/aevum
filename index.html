03 — Answers

Every hesitation,
answered straight.

Is that instant estimate real?
Yes — it's a genuine first-pass from your answers and current carrier rate logic, not a teaser that triples later. It isn't a final offer, because only the carrier sets your exact rate through underwriting, but most healthy applicants land close.
Do I need a medical exam?
Often no. Many carriers now approve healthy applicants through accelerated underwriting using data checks instead of bloodwork — frequently a same-day decision. If you have health conditions, we route you to the product most likely to approve you.
What if I've been turned down before?
You still have options. Simplified-issue and guaranteed-acceptance plans exist specifically for harder-to-insure families — within the eligible age band, guaranteed issue cannot decline you. Our engine routes you there automatically.
How do you get paid?
The carrier pays us a commission — you pay the same premium either way. We're an independent agency, so we shop 40+ carriers for your best fit instead of pushing one company's product.
Which states do you serve?
Florida, Maryland, Virginia and Pennsylvania. Products and rates vary by state and are confirmed by the issuing carrier.
