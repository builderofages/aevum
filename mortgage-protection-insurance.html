Answers

Mortgage Protection Insurance questions.

Does the payout go to the bank?
No — to your named beneficiary, who decides how to use it.
Level or decreasing benefit?
We usually recommend level, so the full amount stays even as the loan drops.
Is a medical exam required?
Not always — no-exam options are common.
What if I refinance or move?
A personally-owned policy stays with you regardless of the loan.
How is it different from PMI?
PMI protects the lender. This protects your family.
